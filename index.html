About Me

So I'm guessing you'd like to know a little something about me? I am a student at UC Berkeley extensions studying in the coding bootcamp program. I currently work fulltime at Saint Mary's Hospital in San Francisco as an O.R. Tech.

Some of my hobbies consist of producing music and sound engineering. I also DJ on the side and collect various vinyl so I can search for samples to use in my music production.

I am a music enthusiast, which means you'll probably see me at multiple music events and shows watching my favorite artists. I also like to collaborate with other music producers to ccreate songs and tracks from scratch.
